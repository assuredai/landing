About Our Initiative

We're catalyzing a national board-level conversation on AI Strategy and Leadership—beginning as a Special Interest Group (SIG) within the Private Directors Association (PDA) and quietly expanding into something more ambitious:

A community-as-a-service platform spearheaded by the stealth newco AssuredAI, designed to equip directors and executives with the tools, insight, and peer network to lead confidently in an AI-transformed world.

What We're Building

The SIG and AssuredAI platform are aligned around a common mission: Helping boards and executives lead strategically, govern responsibly, and act decisively amid accelerating AI adoption and risk.

Our ecosystem includes:

  • Structured frameworks (AI Impact Blueprint, Governance Readiness Diagnostic)
  • Licensable tools and templates for board charters, risk registers, and oversight protocols
  • Board-ready education & intelligence on emerging trends and threats
  • A peer-led, cross-sector community of board members, investors, advisors, and operators

Why This Matters Now

Private company directors are under increasing pressure to understand and oversee AI risk and opportunity—but most lack a trusted network or actionable tools to do so. This initiative bridges that gap.
